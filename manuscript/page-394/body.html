Chapter The One Hundred And Seventh.

(“Lycas,” said he, “these unfortunates upon whom you intend to wreak your vengeance, implore your compassion and have chosen me for this task. I believe that I am a man, by no means unknown, and they desire that, somehow, I will effect a reconciliation between them and their former friends. Surely you do not imagine that these young men fell into such a snare by accident, when the very first thing that concerns every prospective passenger is the name of the captain to whom he intrusts his safety!
